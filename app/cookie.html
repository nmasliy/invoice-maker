What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Applications easily by uninstalling the Applications. You can also request to opt-out via email, at privacy@amabay.app.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Applications and for a reasonable time thereafter. If you’d like us to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via the Applications, please contact us at privacy@amabay.app and we will respond in a reasonable time. Please note that some or all of the User Provided Data may be required in order for the Applications to function properly.

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at privacy@amabay.app. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.
